How to Make Your Candles Last Longer

ðŸ“… November 25, 2025

Did you know that trimming your candle wick can make it last 25% longer? Proper candle care is essential for getting the most out of your Scentoria candles. Always trim the wick to 1/4 inch before each burn and let the wax melt evenly across the surface.

Avoid burning candles for more than 4 hours at a time, and always place them on a heat-resistant surface. These simple tips will help your candles burn cleaner, longer, and safer!

Best Scents for Each Room

ðŸ“… November 20, 2025

Choosing the right scent for each room can transform your home's atmosphere. Lavender and vanilla work wonderfully in bedrooms for relaxation, while citrus and mint scents are perfect for kitchens and bathrooms to keep things fresh.

For living rooms, try warm scents like cinnamon or sandalwood to create a cozy, welcoming environment. Study rooms benefit from eucalyptus or coffee scents that help with focus and concentration.

Why Choose Soy Wax Candles?

ðŸ“… November 15, 2025

Soy wax candles are becoming increasingly popular, and for good reason! Unlike paraffin wax, soy wax is natural, renewable, and eco-friendly. It burns cleaner with less soot, making it better for your health and your home.

Soy candles also last longer than traditional candles and hold fragrance better, giving you more value for your money. At Scentoria, all our candles are made with premium soy wax for the best experience possible.
